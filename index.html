Smugglers Journey

The app is an interactive Star Wars trivia game where users (smugglers) will go on a journey with Han Solo and Chewbacca to deliver a shipment to the end destination. After you have done these tasks, you embark on your journey to complete the mission/put your Star Wars knowledge to the test. You complete 30 questions to test your knowledge of the galaxy far, far away.
